Ingredients

  • Duck
  • Honey
  • Soy sauce
  • Five-spice powder
  • Shallots
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • White vinegar
  • Peking duck pancakes
  • Cucumber
  • Spring onions

Recipe

  1. Clean the duck and remove any excess fat.
  2. Prepare the marinade by mixing honey, soy sauce, five-spice powder, shallots, ginger, garlic, and white vinegar.
  3. Marinate the duck in the sauce for several hours.
  4. Roast the marinated duck in the oven until cooked.
  5. Serve the roasted duck with Peking duck pancakes, cucumber, and spring onions.
