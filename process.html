Step 1: Preparation

Prepare the ingredients and seasonings needed for making Beijing Roast Duck.

Step 2: Marination

Marinate the duck with a special sauce and let it sit for a few hours to absorb the flavors.

Step 3: Roasting

Roast the marinated duck in a special oven until the skin becomes crispy and the meat is tender.
